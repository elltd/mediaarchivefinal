Labyrinth Books

122 Nassau St, Princeton, NJ 08542 | ★★★★☆

A well rounded, modern bookstore offering all the newest releases with a wide range of genres. Conveniently located near many other stores in Princeton. Downsides are that new editions can be a little pricey, and there aren't always sales or deals going on.

Piscataway Public Library - JFK

500 Hoes Ln, Piscataway, NJ 08854 | ★★★☆☆

Items found here mainly consist of CDs as a result of them recently purging their entire physical CD collection. Unfortunately, many of the CDs show a lot of damage as a result of being part of their collection for so long.
