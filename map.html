Music Books Variety

Location types are based on the kinds of media most often found and purchased there.

Station 1 Books & Vinyl

1 Station Plaza, Pompton Lakes, NJ 07442 | ★★★★☆

A cozy store that offers a wide variety of media, including vinyls, CDs, cassette tapes, books, DVDs, figurines, and even cameras and lenses.

Princeton Record Exchange

20 S Tulane St, Princeton, NJ 08542 | ★★★★☆

A store that offers a wide variety of music formats at reasonable prices, ranging from $1 bin finds to the most recent chart-topping releases.

Labyrinth Books

122 Nassau St, Princeton, NJ 08542 | ★★★★☆

A well rounded, spacious, and modern bookstore offering all the newest releases with a wide range of genres. Conveniently located near many other stores in Princeton. Downsides are that new editions can be a little pricey, and there aren't always sales or deals going on.

Piscataway Public Library - JFK

500 Hoes Ln, Piscataway, NJ 08854 | ★★★☆☆

Items found here mainly consist of CDs as a result of them recently purging their entire physical CD collection. Unfortunately, many of the CDs show a lot of damage as a result of being part of their collection for so long.

Mabel Smith Douglass Library

8 Chapel Dr, New Brunswick, NJ 08901 | ★★★☆☆

This library occasionally has a small cart offering free books closer to the closing weeks of school semesters. Offerings have usually included academic journals in a variety of languages, random guides, or other non-fiction.

Barnes & Noble

2130 Route 35, Holmdel, NJ 07733 | ★★★★☆

A standard Barnes and Noble offering both classics and modern books, DVDs and Blu-Rays, vinyls, and more. There is a Criterion Collection shelf here.

Goodwill Store & Donation Center

509 W Union Ave, Bound Brook, NJ 08805 | ★★★☆☆

A standard thrift store. Music and books here have been usually pretty random or in very worn condition, but more luck has been found with DVDs.
