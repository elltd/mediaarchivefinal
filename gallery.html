gallery

We have a wide inventory of items in our media archive— here is the entire overview of each item that has been added and reviewed on this site. This page is updated whenever a new review is added, or whenever possible during the month. If you are looking for a specific kind of item, we have different categories based on the type and format of each entry below. Categories range from books, CDs, to vinyl. Outside of the objects themselves, we also have a list of frequently visited locations we often find items at. Feel free to browse and find something you’ve never seen or heard of before! We are still working to add filtering by genre, artist, or other parameters in the future. Please contact us at about@pma.com if there are any missing entries, if you have any suggestions, or if you are simply curious about an item!

cds

A collection of CDs we currently have. Albums include Charm by Clairo, Hello Nasty by Beastie Boys, and Kind of Blue by Miles Davis.

books

A collection of books we currently have. Books include Birnam Wood by Eleanor Catton, Slaughterhouse-Five by Kurt Vonnegut, and Beautiful Wasps Having Sex by Dori Carter.

cassettes

A collection of cassettes we currently have. Tapes include the Original Motion Picture Soundtrack of Top Gun, The Best of Sheena Easton by Sheena Easton, and the Original Broadway Cast Recording of Bye Bye Birdie.

dvds

A collection of DVDs we currently have. DVDs include Dogfight directed by Nancy Savoca, Rope directed by Alfred Hitchcock, and Collateral directed by Michael Mann.
