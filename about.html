about us

The Physical Media Archive is a web project that was founded in 2025 as a way to collectively document the media we consume. As artists, writers, and creatives, we have an appreciation for physical media in the face of the global shift to digital streaming platforms. Contributing to the Archive is a fun way to share our own interests with others online, while also recording our collections and thoughts! We happily accept reviews from anyone interested in participating. For more information on how to submit reviews to us, click here.
